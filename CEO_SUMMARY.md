# 🏝️ TropiChat: CEO Strategy & Source of Truth

**Last Updated:** March 30, 2026
**Vision:** Become the Caribbean's #1 Social Sales Hub.

---

## 🎯 The Mission
TropiChat is designed to help regional entrepreneurs scale their reach and revenue through modern messaging. We don't just "manage chats"—we turn conversations into a **powered-up sales engine**.

### Core Pillars:
1.  **Maximize Every Conversation:** No lead left behind; every DM is a tracked sales opportunity.
2.  **Unify Social Channels:** WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger in one high-performance dashboard.
3.  **Automate Growth:** 24/7 AI Agents handle pricing, delivery, and common island-specific inquiries.
4.  **Native Caribbean Design:** Built by and for the islands (Bahamas, Jamaica, Trinidad, etc.).

---

## 📊 Business Goals (The North Star)
*   **Target Revenue:** $51,000 ARR (Year 1 Milestone).
*   **Pricing Strategy:** 
    *   **Starter ($29/mo):** "Less than $1/day" for growing businesses.
    *   **Professional ($59/mo):** Most Popular. Outcome-focused with unlimited growth.
*   **The "Moat":** Deep local integration, Meta Verified Partnership status, and "Island-Native" automation (handling regional nuances like island selection and local payment/delivery methods).
